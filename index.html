About

I am a PhD Student at the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP), University of Surrey, under the supervision of Prof. Richard Bowden and Necati Cihan Camgoz. My primary research focus is on Sign Langage Production (SLP), and how Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing can be combined.

Research

My long-term research goal is to break down communication barriers between people, and between computers and people using Natural Language Processing. I believe that by improving how computers understand and respond to different natural languages, more people can benefit from technology.

I am currently motivated by challenges in multilingual NLP including:

  • Context-aware Machine Translation (ACL'21, ACL'21)
  • Sign Language Processing (ECCV'20, COLING'20, ACL'21)
